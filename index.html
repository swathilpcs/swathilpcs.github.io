About Me

Hey there! I’m Swathi, and I’m a Licensed Professional Counselor- Supervisor in the state of Texas (License #76019). A little bit about me: I have lived in Texas since I was 8 years old! I have lived in Austin, Dallas, Belton, Georgetown, and currently live in San Antonio! I graduated with my Master’s in Clinical Mental Health Counseling in 2015 from the University of Mary Hardin-Baylor. I have worked primarily for non-profit organizations, and I also have experience in working in inpatient psychiatric settings as well as outpatient intensive counseling and even private practice for a short period of time! My passion is for serving those who would typically not have access to mental health services. I am currently a doctoral student at The University of Texas at San Antonio, and anticipating graduating with my PhD in May 2025 in Counselor Education and Supervision. I live in San Antonio with my partner and our two mini golden-doodles, who are featured on this site! In our free time, we enjoy exploring new coffee shops and restaurants as well as being outdoors for live music! We also cherish our time with our family and close friends.
